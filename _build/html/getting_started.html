0. Getting Started¶

How Pixel Strings differs¶

Traditionally, Cinnafilm’s Tachyon and Dark Energy technologies could only be used through expensive third-party transcoders. But with the power of Pixel Strings, these capabilities have been brought forward to everyone.

The Process¶

Like a typical transcoder, Pixel Strings works in a 3-part process.

  1. Upload media
    • This is your ingest process. You footage goes into the cloud, where we do all of our processing.
  2. Convert Media
    • This is where the magic happens. You choose your output framerate and resolution, and we do the rest.
  3. Delivery
    • Deliver your output media back to a cloud service like AWS, or simply download the file locally.
